Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

Welcome to ChildCare AI. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the ChildCare AI mobile application (the "App") and website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and physical address.
  • Child Data: Name, age, allergies, and medical notes provided by parents for care management.
  • Center Data: License numbers, capacity, and facility details provided by care centers.
  • Usage Data: Information about how the App is accessed and used.

2. Use of Data

ChildCare AI uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service.
  • To manage bookings between parents and childcare providers.
  • To provide AI-powered insights for child safety and center efficiency.
  • To provide customer support and notify you about changes to our Service.

3. Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us. We use industry-standard encryption and secure protocols (SSL/TLS) to protect your information. However, remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our Service. These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service involves collecting data about children, but only as provided by their parents or legal guardians for the purpose of childcare management. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
